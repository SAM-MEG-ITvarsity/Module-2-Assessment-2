Our Story

Gravity Trampoline Park is a premier destination for family fun and active entertainment. Founded with the vision of providing an exhilarating and safe environment for all ages, Gravity Trampoline Park offers a wide range of activities including wall-to-wall trampolines, dodgeball, foam pits, and more. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to creating memorable experiences for our guests through innovative attractions and exceptional service.

Our Mission

Our mission at Gravity Trampoline Park is to deliver high-energy fun and fitness opportunities in a safe and engaging environment. We strive to offer a unique blend of physical activity and entertainment that appeals to individuals and families alike. Our goal is to inspire a love for active play while ensuring that everyone who visits has a fantastic time and leaves with a smile.

Our Values

  • Safety: We prioritize the safety and well-being of our guests, with rigorous safety protocols and equipment maintenance.
  • Fun: Our main objective is to provide an enjoyable experience for everyone through exciting and interactive attractions.
  • Customer Service: We are committed to delivering outstanding customer service, ensuring that every guest feels welcomed and valued.
  • Innovation: We continually seek to enhance our facilities and attractions to offer the latest in entertainment and fitness trends.
  • Community: We aim to foster a sense of community and encourage active lifestyles through our programs and events.
