Our Story

Mr Price is a leading retail chain known for offering stylish, quality clothing, home decor, and more at affordable prices. Founded in 1985, Mr Price has grown into a prominent name in retail with a focus on providing value for money without compromising on style. Our stores are designed to make fashion and home essentials accessible to everyone, providing a range of products that cater to modern lifestyles.

Our Mission

At Mr Price, our mission is to deliver exceptional value to our customers by offering high-quality products at competitive prices. We aim to make fashion and home living accessible and enjoyable, ensuring that every shopping experience is pleasant and satisfying. Our commitment is to provide the best value for money while staying on top of the latest trends and innovations.
