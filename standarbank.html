Our Story

Standard Bank, founded in 1862, is one of Africa’s largest and most trusted financial institutions. With a rich history spanning over 150 years, we offer a comprehensive range of banking and financial services to individuals, businesses, and institutions. Our mission is to provide innovative financial solutions, excellent customer service, and support sustainable economic growth across Africa. We are dedicated to helping our clients achieve their financial goals with integrity and professionalism.

Our Mission

At Standard Bank, our mission is to be the leading African financial institution by delivering exceptional banking services and solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients. We aim to build lasting relationships based on trust and performance, while driving positive economic and social impact across the continent. Our commitment is to provide secure, reliable, and innovative financial solutions that contribute to the growth and prosperity of our clients and communities.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards and act with transparency and honesty in all our dealings.
  • Customer Focus: We prioritize our clients’ needs and work diligently to provide personalized and effective solutions.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of our business, aiming to exceed expectations and deliver outstanding results.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovation and continuously seek new ways to improve our services and stay ahead in the financial sector.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to sustainable practices that support economic development and benefit our communities and environment.
