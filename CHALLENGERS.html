Synopsis: "Challengers" is a dramatic sports film that follows the journey of a rising tennis star, Tashi (Zendaya), who faces intense personal and professional challenges. As Tashi navigates the world of competitive tennis, she must confront her past and deal with complex relationships, including a love triangle involving her husband, Art (Mike Faist), and a former lover, Rishi (Josh O'Connor). The film delves into themes of ambition, betrayal, and redemption, offering a compelling narrative set against the backdrop of high-stakes tennis tournaments.
