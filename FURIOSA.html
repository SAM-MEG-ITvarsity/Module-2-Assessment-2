Furiosa

Details

Main Actors: Anya Taylor-Joy, Chris Hemsworth

Age Restriction: R

Synopsis: "Furiosa" is a prequel to the "Mad Max" franchise, focusing on the origin story of the iconic character Furiosa (Anya Taylor-Joy). Set in a dystopian future, the film explores Furiosa's rise to power and her quest for redemption amidst the chaos of a post-apocalyptic world. Alongside a charismatic ally (Chris Hemsworth), she battles ruthless warlords and treacherous terrain in a high-stakes journey for survival and justice.
