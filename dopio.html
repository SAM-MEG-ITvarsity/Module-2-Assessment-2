Our Story

Doppio Zero is a celebrated South African restaurant chain renowned for its vibrant atmosphere and delectable cuisine. Founded in 2002, Doppio Zero specializes in offering a diverse menu that includes freshly baked pizzas, artisanal pastas, gourmet burgers, and decadent desserts. Our restaurants are designed to provide a warm and inviting dining experience where guests can enjoy high-quality food in a relaxed setting, perfect for both casual outings and special occasions.

Our Mission

At Doppio Zero, our mission is to deliver an exceptional dining experience characterized by delicious food, outstanding service, and a welcoming atmosphere. We are committed to using the finest ingredients and innovative culinary techniques to create dishes that delight our guests. Our goal is to foster a community-centered dining environment where everyone feels at home and enjoys every meal to the fullest.

Our Values

  • Quality: We prioritize high-quality ingredients and expert preparation to ensure every dish is exceptional.
  • Innovation: We continuously innovate our menu and offerings to provide fresh and exciting dining experiences.
  • Customer Experience: We strive to deliver top-notch service and create memorable experiences for every guest.
  • Community: We believe in fostering a sense of community and supporting local initiatives.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to sustainable practices and minimizing our environmental impact.
