Our Story

Edgars is one of South Africa’s most iconic retail brands, renowned for its diverse range of fashion, beauty, and home products. Established in 1929, Edgars has built a legacy of offering quality merchandise and exceptional service. Our stores are a destination for those seeking the latest trends in fashion, beauty products, and home essentials, all in one convenient location.

Our Mission

Our mission at Edgars is to provide our customers with a premier shopping experience by offering a comprehensive selection of high-quality products across fashion, beauty, and home categories. We aim to deliver outstanding value, style, and service, ensuring that every customer leaves our store satisfied and inspired.

Our Values

  • Customer Focus: We are committed to understanding and meeting our customers’ needs and preferences.
  • Quality: We offer products that meet the highest standards of quality and style.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovation to enhance our product offerings and shopping experience.
  • Community: We support and contribute to the communities we operate in.
