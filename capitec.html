Our Story

Capitec Bank, established in 2001, is a leading South African bank known for its innovative approach to banking. We pride ourselves on offering simple, affordable, and accessible banking solutions to individuals and businesses. With a focus on transparency and customer-centricity, Capitec aims to make banking as easy and convenient as possible. Our commitment to technology and efficiency has helped us grow into one of the most trusted and respected banks in the country.

Our Mission

Our mission at Capitec Bank is to provide straightforward banking solutions that enhance our customers' financial well-being. We strive to deliver exceptional service and innovative products that meet the evolving needs of our clients. Our goal is to empower people by making banking more accessible, affordable, and efficient, thereby contributing to their financial success and improving their quality of life.

Our Values

  • Customer Focus: We prioritize our customers' needs and aim to provide services that exceed their expectations.
  • Simplicity: We believe in simplifying banking processes to make financial management easy and stress-free.
  • Integrity: We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our operations.
  • Innovation: We embrace technology and innovation to continuously improve our services and solutions.
  • Efficiency: We focus on efficiency to deliver fast and effective banking solutions for our clients.
