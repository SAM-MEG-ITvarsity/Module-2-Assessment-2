Our Story

First National Bank (FNB), founded in 1838, is one of South Africa's oldest and most trusted banks. With a rich heritage spanning over 180 years, FNB has evolved to become a leader in banking innovation and customer service. We are dedicated to providing a comprehensive range of banking and financial solutions, leveraging cutting-edge technology to meet the diverse needs of our customers. FNB's commitment to excellence and integrity has solidified its position as a cornerstone of the South African financial sector.

Our Mission

At FNB, our mission is to deliver innovative financial solutions that empower our customers and enhance their lives. We aim to offer a seamless banking experience through our extensive range of products and services, all while maintaining the highest standards of customer service. Our goal is to be the bank of choice by delivering value and building lasting relationships based on trust and reliability.

Our Values

  • Customer Centricity: We place our customers at the heart of everything we do, focusing on their needs and expectations.
  • Innovation: We are committed to leading the way in banking technology and digital solutions to provide a superior customer experience.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in our operations and interactions, fostering trust and transparency.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of our business, from service delivery to product development.
  • Collaboration: We work together to achieve our goals and build strong relationships with our customers, employees, and stakeholders.
