Our Story

Spur is a renowned South African restaurant chain celebrated for its warm hospitality and family-friendly atmosphere. Established in 1967, Spur has grown to become a favorite dining destination, known for its mouth-watering steaks, burgers, and ribs. Our restaurants offer a vibrant and relaxed setting where guests of all ages can enjoy hearty meals and create lasting memories with family and friends.

Our Mission

At Spur, our mission is to provide an exceptional dining experience with a focus on quality, taste, and customer satisfaction. We are dedicated to serving delicious, flame-grilled meals in a welcoming environment that makes every visit special. Our goal is to continually exceed our guests' expectations by delivering outstanding service and maintaining high standards in everything we do.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the freshest ingredients to ensure that every meal meets our high standards of taste and quality.
  • Family: We create a family-friendly environment where everyone feels welcome and valued.
  • Customer Service: We are committed to delivering excellent service and making every guest's experience enjoyable.
  • Community: We are dedicated to being an active part of the community and contributing positively to the local area.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency, building trust with our customers and partners.
