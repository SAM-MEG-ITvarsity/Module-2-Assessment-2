Synopsis: "Elemental" is an animated adventure set in a world where the elements of earth, water, air, and fire live in harmony. When a disruptive force threatens to upset the balance, a group of elemental beings, including Ember (Zendaya) and Blaze (John Cena), must unite to restore order. This visually stunning film combines action, humor, and heart, exploring themes of unity and environmental stewardship.
