About Nu-Metro Cinemas

Nu-Metro is a premier cinema chain offering state-of-the-art movie experiences across various locations. Known for its luxury seating, high-quality sound systems, and the latest in film technology, Nu-Metro provides a top-notch viewing experience that caters to all movie enthusiasts. With a commitment to customer satisfaction, Nu-Metro ensures that every visit is a memorable one.

  • Gourmet Snacks: Savor a variety of premium snacks and beverages, including artisanal popcorn and craft sodas.
  • Exclusive Lounges: Relax in exclusive lounges with access to VIP services and amenities.
  • Online Booking: Conveniently book your tickets online and choose your preferred seats in advance.
