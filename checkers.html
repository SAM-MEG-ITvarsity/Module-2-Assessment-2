Our Story

Checkers is a leading supermarket chain committed to offering great value and a broad range of quality products. Established in 1991, Checkers has grown rapidly and become a staple in the retail industry, known for its extensive product range, excellent customer service, and competitive pricing. We are dedicated to enhancing your shopping experience with convenience and value at every visit.

Our Mission

At Checkers, our mission is to deliver exceptional value to our customers by providing high-quality products at affordable prices. We strive to create a shopping environment that is convenient and enjoyable, ensuring that our customers receive the best service and value for their money. Our commitment is to be the preferred destination for all your grocery needs.

Our Values

  • Customer Satisfaction: We prioritize our customers' needs and aim to exceed their expectations.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency and honesty in all our dealings.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek to improve our offerings and enhance the shopping experience.
  • Community Focus: We are dedicated to making a positive impact in the communities we serve.
