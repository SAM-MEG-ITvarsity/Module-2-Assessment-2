Our Story

Virgin Active is a leading global fitness brand that is dedicated to helping you live a healthier and more active life. Founded in 1999, Virgin Active offers state-of-the-art gym facilities, innovative fitness classes, and expert trainers to support your fitness journey. With a focus on community and wellness, Virgin Active creates vibrant environments where members can achieve their fitness goals and enjoy a variety of wellness programs.

Our Mission

At Virgin Active, our mission is to inspire and empower individuals to embrace a healthier lifestyle through exceptional fitness facilities and innovative programs. We are committed to providing a supportive and motivating environment where members can achieve their personal fitness goals, improve their overall well-being, and enjoy a sense of community.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, from our facilities to our services and staff.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek innovative ways to enhance the fitness experience and deliver cutting-edge programs.
  • Inclusivity: We welcome everyone and foster an inclusive environment that supports all fitness levels and goals.
  • Wellness: We promote holistic wellness by offering a range of fitness, health, and relaxation services.
  • Community: We build a strong sense of community through engaging activities and supportive interactions.
