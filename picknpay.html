Our Story

Pick n Pay is one of South Africa's largest and most respected supermarket chains. Established in 1967, Pick n Pay has grown to become a leader in retail, offering a wide variety of products ranging from groceries and fresh produce to household items and more. Our stores are known for their commitment to quality, value, and customer service, providing an exceptional shopping experience for all our customers.

Our Mission

At Pick n Pay, our mission is to provide the best possible shopping experience by offering a broad selection of high-quality products at competitive prices. We are dedicated to making grocery shopping easy and enjoyable, with a focus on customer satisfaction and community engagement. Our goal is to continuously improve and innovate to meet the evolving needs of our customers.

Our Values

  • Customer Centricity: We put our customers at the heart of everything we do.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of our business.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovation to enhance our services and offerings.
  • Community: We are committed to supporting and giving back to the communities we serve.
